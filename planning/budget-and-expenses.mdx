## Setting your total budget

Select **Edit budget** icon and enter the total amount you'd like to spend. You can change this anytime as your plans evolve.

## Organizing with categories

Group expenses into categories — venue, catering, attire, and so on — each with its own color so your budget chart is easy to read at a glance. Add, rename, or reorder categories by dragging them.

## Adding expenses

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a category">
    Or create a new one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select New expense">
    Give it a name and amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose fixed or per-person">
    A per-person expense (like catering) automatically scales with your guest count.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Your budget uses the currency set in [Wedding settings, plan & billing](/planning/wedding-settings-plan-and-billing). If you change your currency later, you'll be offered the option to convert existing amounts.
</Tip>

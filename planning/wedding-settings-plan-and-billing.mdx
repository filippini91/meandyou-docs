## Wedding details

Update your wedding's **name** and **preferred currency** here. If you change currency after adding budget items, you'll be offered the option to convert existing amounts to the new currency.

## Inviting co-planners

Under **Users**, select **Invite** and enter an email address to bring your partner or anyone else into the planning process. They'll get an email invitation and, once accepted, can see and edit your wedding just like you can (with the exception of billing, which stays with the owner). You can remove a member or cancel a pending invite anytime.

## Your trial, plan, and billing

Every new wedding starts with a **30-day free trial** — full access to every feature, no credit card needed. When the trial ends, continue with a **single one-time payment**. There’s no subscription and no plan tiers to choose from.

<Note>
  If your trial ends before you purchase, you can still open your wedding, but most features stay locked until you complete payment.
</Note>

From this page you can check your trial status, unlock full access, manage your payment method, and download receipts.

## Deleting your wedding

Only the wedding's owner can delete it, and you'll need to type a confirmation phrase first.

<Warning>
  Deleting a wedding removes all of its data — guests, tasks, budget, website, everything — and can't be undone.
</Warning>

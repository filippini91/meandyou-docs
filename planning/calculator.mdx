Not sure how much wine, food, or favors to order? **Calculator** estimates quantities based on your guest counts, so you're not guessing.

<Frame>
  ![Calculator page with a list of items and their calculated quantities based on guest count](/images/planning/calculator-01.png)
</Frame>

{/* TODO(screenshot): Calculator page with 2-3 sample items (e.g. wine, main course) and their calculated totals */}

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select New item">
    Name it (for example, "Red wine") and choose a unit — kilograms, liters, or pieces.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose how it's calculated">
    Base it on adults, children, a specific guest tag, or a custom count — whichever fits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the total">
    Calculator does the math for you based on your current guest list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The more complete your [guest list](/guest-experience/building-your-guest-list) is, the more accurate these estimates will be.
</Tip>

Once you're happy with your list, select **Download** to export it as a PDF — handy to bring shopping or to share with a caterer.

## Adding a pin

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select New pin">
    Choose the type: **URL**, **Photo**, or **Note**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your content">
    Paste a link, upload a photo, or write a quick note about an idea.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Your pin appears on the board, ready to browse anytime.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Finding and removing pins

Use the search bar to find a specific pin as your board grows. Select any pin and choose **Delete** to remove it — removing a pin doesn't affect anything elsewhere in your wedding plan.

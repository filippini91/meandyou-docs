## Getting-started checklist

A short list of first steps — things like setting up Wedding settings, exploring Inspiration, adding guests, setting a budget, and adding tasks. Each item links straight to the relevant page. It's meant to be a starting point, not a strict order.

## Countdown

A live countdown to your wedding date. If you haven't set a date yet, select it here — your date also drives task deadlines and your guest-facing schedule, so it's worth adding early.

<Note>
  Changing your wedding date after you've already added tasks may prompt you to recalculate their due dates to match the new date.
</Note>

## Unread messages

If anyone planning with you has sent a message you haven't seen, you'll see an alert here linking to [Messages](/planning/messages).

## Your vendors

Keep a running list of the people and businesses helping bring your wedding to life — venue, catering, photographer, and more.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Add vendor">
    Choose a category (venue, catering, photography, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    Name, contact information, and any notes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Your vendor appears in the list, and venues you add here can also be reused on your wedding website and invitation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

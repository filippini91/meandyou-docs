sidebarTitle: "Resetting a password"
description: "How to recover your account with a password reset link if you can't remember your password."
icon: "lock-keyhole"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Forgot your password?">
    On the sign-in page, select **Forgot your password?**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email">
    Type the email address you use for me&you and select **Send reset link**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email">
    You'll see a **Check your email** message. If an account exists with that address, we'll send instructions to reset your password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the link and set a new password">
    Click the link in the email, then enter and confirm a new password on the **Reset Password** screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  This is different from changing your password while you're already signed in. To do that instead, see [Password & security](/your-account/password-and-security).
</Note>

If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder, and make sure you're using the same email address you originally signed up with.

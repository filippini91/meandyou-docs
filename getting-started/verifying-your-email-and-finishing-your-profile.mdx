## Confirming your email

If you signed up with email and password, we send a confirmation link to your inbox right away. You'll see a **"Thank you for signing up!"** message asking you to check your email.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the confirmation email">
    Look for an email from me&you. Check your spam folder if you don't see it within a few minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the confirmation link">
    This takes you back to me&you and confirms your email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    You can now sign in with the password you chose when signing up.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you signed up with Google, your email is already verified — you can skip this part.
</Note>

## Finishing your profile

The first time you sign in, we'll ask you to complete your profile before you can continue:

- **First name** and **last name** (required)
- **Profile picture** (optional) — drag a photo in, or click to upload. PNG, JPG, HEIC or HEIF up to 5MB.

## Sign in with email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the sign in page">
    Open me&you and select **Sign In**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email and password">
    Type the email and password you used to create your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Sign In">
    You'll be taken straight to your wedding plan.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sign in with Google

If you originally signed up with Google, select **Continue with Google** on the sign-in page and choose your account.

## If sign-in doesn't work

- **"Invalid login credentials"** — double-check your email and password. If you're not sure, use **Forgot your password?** to set a new one (see [Resetting a forgotten password](/getting-started/resetting-a-forgotten-password)).
- **"Email has not been confirmed"** — you signed up but haven't clicked the confirmation link yet. Check your inbox, or see [Verifying your email & finishing your profile](/getting-started/verifying-your-email-and-finishing-your-profile).

<Tip>
  Don't have an account yet? Go to [Creating your account](/getting-started/creating-your-account).
</Tip>

The **questionnaire** is how you let the couple know you're coming (or not), plus any other details they've asked for — like your meal choice.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your guest portal">
    Use the link from your invitation email, or sign in — see [Finding your invitation](/for-your-guests/finding-your-invitation).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Fill in">
    Next to the questionnaire section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Answer the questions">
    These vary by wedding, but often include whether you're attending, meal preferences, and any notes for the couple.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your answers">
    You're all set — the couple will see your response right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Changing your answer later

Plans changed? Open your guest portal again and select **Update** or **Edit** next to the questionnaire to revise your answers anytime before the wedding.

## RSVPing for your whole party

If you're bringing a plus-one, partner, or children, they may already be listed under your invitation — you can usually answer for everyone in your party from the same screen. Besides filling out their RSVP, you'll also need to fill out their name.

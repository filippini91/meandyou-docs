Want to make sure your favorite song gets played? If the couple has turned on the music wishlist, you can suggest it yourself.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your guest portal">
    Find the **Music wishlist** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for a song">
    Type a song or artist name to search.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add it to the wishlist">
    Select the song from the results to add it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Voting on other suggestions

Select the heart icon next to a song someone else added if you'd love to hear it too — popular songs rise to the top, giving the couple a good sense of the crowd favorites.

Added a song by mistake? You can remove your own suggestions anytime.

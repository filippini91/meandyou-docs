If the couple has set one up, their wedding website is a great place to find everything you need to know before the big day — no sign-in required.

## How to get there

Use the link the couple shared with you (often in their invitation email, a text, or a QR code), or select **Website** from your guest portal.

## What you might find there

- The couple's story and a welcome message
- Venue details and the date and time
- A full schedule of the day
- A photo gallery
- A countdown to the wedding
- A button to fill in the questionnaire (RSVP)
- Contact details if you have questions
- A button to upload your own photos and videos

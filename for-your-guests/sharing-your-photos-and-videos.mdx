If the couple has turned on media uploads, you can share your own photos and videos from the wedding — no more scattered group chats.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the upload link">
    Look for an **Upload** button on the wedding website, a QR code at the venue, or **Upload media** in your guest portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    You'll need to sign in with your email — this just helps keep track of who shared what.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your files">
    Drag and drop, or select files from your device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse the gallery">
    See everything you've uploaded under **Your files**, or browse everyone's photos under **All files**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Changed your mind about a photo? You can remove anything you've personally uploaded while logged in at any time.

Been invited to a wedding through me&you? Here's how to find everything you need.

## If you have the email

Look for an email from the couple with a personal link — selecting it takes you straight to your **guest portal**, where you can view your invitation and RSVP. No account or sign-in required.

## If you can't find the email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the guest portal sign-in page">
    Visit `meandyou.wedding/guest-portal`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Use the same email address the couple invited you with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your invitation">
    If you have more than one invitation, you'll see a list — choose the right one. If you only have one, you'll be taken straight there.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Still can't find it? Reach out to the couple directly — they can resend your invitation email.
</Tip>

## What you'll find in your guest portal

Depending on what the couple has turned on, you might see a countdown, the questionnaire (RSVP), a way to view their invitation, carpool offers, a song wishlist, a preview of the schedule, and a feed of updates.

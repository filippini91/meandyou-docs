The **feed** is a shared space for updates about the wedding — think of it like a private social feed just for this event.

## Reading updates

Open the feed from your guest portal to see posts, photos, and polls the couple has shared.

## Posting (if enabled)

Some couples allow guests to post too. If you see a **New post** option:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Write your message">
    Add text, and a photo or video if you'd like.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post">
    Your update appears in the feed for everyone to see.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you don't see a way to post, the couple has kept the feed read-only for guests — you can still read updates and vote on polls.
</Note>

## Voting on polls

If the couple posts a poll, select an option to cast your vote. You won't be able to create your own polls — that's reserved for the couple.

Your digital invitation is what guests see first — a styled card with your names, venues, date and time, and a button that takes them to your questionnaire.

## Designing your invitation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Turn the invitation On">
    Found at the top of the Invite page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your details">
    Couple names, venue(s), date and time, and any text you'd like above or below the main details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a style">
    Pick a layout variant, a color, and a background — a pattern, image, or solid color.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Changes save automatically as you go.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Use the live preview to see exactly what guests will see before you send anything.

## Sharing it

Select **Get unique link for** to grab a shareable link, or send it directly to your guest list — see [Building your guest list](/guest-experience/building-your-guest-list) for sending RSVP emails, which include a link to both your invitation and your questionnaire.

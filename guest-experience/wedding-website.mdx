Your wedding website is a public page you can share with guests — it can include your story, venue details, schedule, photos, and a way to RSVP.

## Turning it on

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Website">
    Under **Guest management** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Turn the toggle On">
    Your site becomes visible at your chosen link.

    <Frame>
      ![23 Website Switch](/images/23_website_switch.jpg)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your URL">
    Edit the address slug so it's easy for guests to remember and type. Change the slug, see whether it's available and it gets saved automatically after you're done.

    <Frame>
      <video src="/videos/23_website_slugChange.mp4" controls />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a look">
    Choose a color scheme and font pairing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Designing your pages

Select **Edit website** to open the visual editor. Add and reorder sections like:

- A hero section with your names and a welcome message
- Where & when (your venues, date, and time)
- Schedule (pulled from [Schedule & timeline](/planning/schedule-and-timeline))
- A photo gallery
- A countdown to your big day
- An RSVP button linking to your questionnaire
- Contact cards for questions
- A media upload button so guests can share their own photos

Select **Save** as you go, and use **Open website** anytime to preview exactly what your guests will see.

<Tip>
  Not ready to share it yet? Leave the toggle off — your site stays private until you turn it on.
</Tip>

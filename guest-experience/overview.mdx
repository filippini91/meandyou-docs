Everything in this section is about the experience your **guests** have — from receiving an invitation to RSVPing to enjoying your wedding website. It all starts in the same place.

## Where to start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Build your guest list">
    Add or import everyone you're inviting. See [Building your guest list](/guest-experience/building-your-guest-list).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up how you'll invite people">
    Design your [digital invitation](/guest-experience/digital-invitation) and/or build your [questionnaire](/guest-experience/questionnaire) — you need at least one of these active before you can send RSVP emails.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Turn on your wedding website (optional but popular)">
    See [Your wedding website](/guest-experience/wedding-website).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send your invitations">
    From your guest list, select **Send emails** to email your guests.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Keep guests updated as the date gets closer">
    Use [Announcements](/guest-experience/announcements) for one-off updates, or turn on the [guest feed](/guest-experience/guest-feed) for ongoing posts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Turn responses into a seating chart">
    Once RSVPs start coming in, build your [seating chart](/guest-experience/seating-chart).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your wedding website, digital invitation, questionnaire, guest media uploads, and announcements all require a paid plan (Plus or Premium) after your trial ends. Your guest list, feed, and seating chart are always available.
</Note>

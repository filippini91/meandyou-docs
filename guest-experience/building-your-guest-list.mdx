## Adding guests

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Add guest">
    Enter a name and, ideally, an email address so you can send them an invitation later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add companions if needed">
    Group a plus-one, partner, or children under the same invitation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tag guests (optional)">
    Use tags like "family," "friends," or "work" to organize and filter your list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Importing a list

Already have a spreadsheet? Select **Import** and upload a CSV file to add many guests at once.

## Keeping your list organized

Use search, choose which columns to display, and select multiple guests to delete or update them together. Each guest's row shows their RSVP status at a glance, and selecting a guest lets you edit their details, view their questionnaire answers, add a companion, resend an email, or remove them.

## Guest settings

The **Guest settings** tab (next to Overview) is where you turn on and configure the modules guests can access — your website, invitation, questionnaire, feed, and media uploads. Each of those has its own guide in this section.

<Tip>
  Haven't sent invitations yet? Look for the **Unsent RSVPs** prompt on the Guests page — it's a quick way to catch anyone you haven't emailed yet.
</Tip>

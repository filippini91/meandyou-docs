## Adding guests

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Add guest">
    Enter a name and, ideally, an email address so you can send them an invitation later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add companions if needed">
    Group a plus-one, partner, or children under the same invitation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tag guests (optional)">
    Use tags like "family," "friends," or "work" to organize and filter your list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Importing a list

Already have a spreadsheet? Select **Import** and upload a CSV file to add many guests at once.

## Keeping your list organized

Use search, choose which columns to display, and select multiple guests to delete or update them together. Each guest's row shows their RSVP status at a glance, and selecting a guest lets you edit their details, view their questionnaire answers, add a companion, resend an email, or remove them.

## Guest settings

The **Guest settings** tab (next to Overview) is where you turn on and configure the modules guests can access — your website, invitation, questionnaire, feed, and media uploads. Each of those has its own guide in this section.

Take a good look at these settings to use me&you to the fullest.

## Sending emails with smart send

Look for the **Send emails to your guests** card on the Guests page — it's a quick way to catch anyone you haven't emailed yet. Select **Send emails** and our smart send system takes care of the rest: it looks at where each guest is in the RSVP process and automatically picks the right email for them, so nobody gets the wrong message or an accidental duplicate.

<Frame>
  ![22 Guests Send Emails](/images/22_guests_sendEmails.jpg)
</Frame>

### The emails smart send can send

- **Initial invitation** — the first email to a guest you haven't contacted yet. It includes your questionnaire if you've turned one on.
- **Questionnaire added** — lets a guest know a questionnaire is now ready for them, if you invited them before it existed.
- **Reminder to complete** — for guests who were invited but haven't answered your questionnaire yet.
- **Review after an update** — for guests who already answered, sent when you've added or changed questions since their response.

### Who smart send skips

- Guests without an email address — add one to their details to include them next time.
- Guests who've already finished and nothing has changed since.
- Anyone emailed in the last 24 hours, so you can't accidentally send the same guest two emails in a row.

Select **What emails will we send?** on the card to preview exactly how many guests fall into each case before you send.

<Tip>
  Companions who share one email address always get a single email with one shared link — never separate emails. And you'll need at least one of your [digital invitation](/guest-experience/digital-invitation) or [questionnaire](/guest-experience/questionnaire) turned on before smart send has anything to do.
</Tip>

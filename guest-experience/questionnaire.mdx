The **questionnaire** is what me&you calls your RSVP form — the questions guests answer when they respond to your invitation. It can be as simple as "Will you attend?" or as detailed as meal choices and song requests.

## Building your questionnaire

<Steps>
  <Step title="Turn the questionnaire On">
    Found at the top of the Questionnaire page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Add question">
    Choose a type: free text, single choice, or multiple choice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your question and options">
    For choice questions, add each option guests can pick from.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reorder and save">
    Drag questions into the order you'd like, and show or hide any as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sending it to guests

From your [guest list](/guest-experience/building-your-guest-list), select **Send RSVP** to email your guests a link to fill it in. You can also copy a link for an individual guest if you'd rather send it yourself.

## Reading responses

Answers show up back on your guest list — each guest's row reflects their RSVP status and answers, so you can see at a glance who's responded and who hasn't.

<Tip>
  You need either an active invitation or an active questionnaire before you can send RSVP emails — having both is common, since the invitation links straight into the questionnaire.
</Tip>

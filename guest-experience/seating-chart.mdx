Skip the paper chart and sticky notes — **Seating chart** is a drag-and-drop floor plan for assigning guests to tables.

  ![Seating chart canvas with round and rectangular tables, some seats filled with guest names](/images/guest-experience/seating-01.png)
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a table">
    Choose a shape — round, square, or rectangular — and give it a name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drag guests onto seats">
    Pick guests from the list on the side and drop them onto an open seat.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust as needed">
    Rotate or reposition tables, and zoom in or out to work on a large chart comfortably.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best time to start

Seating works best once RSVPs have started coming in through your [questionnaire](/guest-experience/questionnaire), so you're working with confirmed numbers rather than your full invite list.

## Exporting

Select **Download** to export your seating chart and guest list as a PDF or ZIP — useful to share with your venue or caterer.

<Note>
  Announcements are available on paid plans, after your trial ends.
</Note>

**Announcements** let you email all of your guests at once — a change of venue, parking instructions, or a heartfelt thank-you after the big day.

<Frame>
  ![Announcements page showing a compose box with subject and message, and a history of previously sent messages](/images/guest-experience/announcements-01.png)
</Frame>

{/* TODO(screenshot): Announcements page with the New message compose view and a sent-history list below */}

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select New message">
    Write a subject line and your message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your recipients">
    Send to everyone on your guest list, or select specific guests.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post to the feed too (optional)">
    Turn this on to also share the update on your [guest feed](/guest-experience/guest-feed).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Your message goes out by email right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reviewing past announcements

Every announcement you've sent stays in your history, and you can select **Resend** to send it again — handy for reminders.

<Tip>
  Announcements only reach guests who have an email address on file, so it's worth double-checking your [guest list](/guest-experience/building-your-guest-list) is complete first.
</Tip>

Open **Account Settings** → **Notifications**. Every toggle here saves instantly — there's no separate save button.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Unread messages summary** | A recap of unread messages in your weddings, sent every morning at 7:00. |
| **Red task alerts** | A heads-up when a task has fewer than 3 days left before its deadline. |
| **Today's tasks** | A reminder for any tasks due today. |
| **Member invitation accepted** | Only for wedding owners — an alert when someone accepts your invitation to co-plan. |
